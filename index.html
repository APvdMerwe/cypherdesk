Acquiring and Tracking Made Simple

Cypherdesk is the ideal solution for clients who want to simplify the process of securing and tracking their vehicles. With our platform, clients can create and track job cards, ensuring that their security and tracking devices are installed and maintained efficiently and effectively. Additionally, our platform provides real-time updates, making it easy for clients to stay informed about the status of their installations.

you can take it anywhere

Take your security and tracking on the go with CypherDesk! Our platform is fully optimized for multiple devices, so you can access your information from anywhere, at any time. Whether you're using a computer, tablet, or smartphone, you can stay informed and in control of our multiple features.

  • Track customer details.
    View all customer details, associated vehicles, order details, and job card details in one convenient location.
  • Track orders.
    Track your orders and job cards in real-time with the CypherDesk Order Tracking.
  • View job cards.
    Quickly view job cards, complete with all the relevant details, including job card number and installer details.
  • Track your vehicles.
    Track your vehicles and their installations with the CypherDesk Vehicle section.
  • Manage and configure tracking devices.
    Stay informed about your tracking devices with the CypherDesk Tracking Device section.
  • Generate reports.
    Stay informed and make informed decisions with the CypherDesk Reporting.
